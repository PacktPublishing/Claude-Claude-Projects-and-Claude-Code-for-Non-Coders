# TrendyFinds Shipping Quick Reference Guide

## Shipping Cutoff Times (EST)

### Same-Day Processing
- Standard/Express: Order by 2 PM EST
- Overnight: Order by 12 PM EST
- International: Order by 10 AM EST
- Friday orders: Ship same day if before cutoff
- Weekend orders: Ship Monday

## Carrier Service Levels

### FedEx
**FedEx Ground (Standard)**
- Transit: 5-7 business days
- Tracking updates: Every 12-24 hours
- Saturday delivery: No
- Signature: Not required

**FedEx 2Day (Express)**
- Transit: 2 business days
- Tracking updates: Every 4-6 hours
- Saturday delivery: Yes (extra fee)
- Signature: Optional

**FedEx Standard Overnight**
- Transit: Next business day by 3 PM
- Tracking updates: Real-time
- Saturday delivery: Yes (extra fee)
- Signature: Required for >$500

### USPS
**USPS Ground Advantage**
- Transit: 5-7 business days
- Tracking updates: Major hubs only
- Saturday delivery: Yes
- Insurance: Up to $100 included

**USPS Priority Mail**
- Transit: 1-3 business days
- Tracking updates: Each scan point
- Saturday delivery: Yes
- Insurance: Up to $100 included

### UPS
**UPS Ground**
- Transit: 5-7 business days
- Saturday delivery: No
- Best for: East Coast destinations

**UPS Next Day Air**
- Transit: Next business day by 10:30 AM
- Saturday delivery: Available
- Best for: Time-sensitive orders

## International Shipping

### Zones & Transit Times
**Zone 1: Canada**
- Transit: 7-10 business days
- Customs: Usually 1-2 days
- Duties: Customer responsible

**Zone 2: Mexico & Caribbean**
- Transit: 10-12 business days
- Customs: 2-4 days typical
- Restrictions: Check textile limits

**Zone 3: Europe**
- Transit: 10-14 business days
- Customs: 2-5 days typical
- VAT: Customer pays on delivery

**Zone 4: Asia-Pacific**
- Transit: 12-16 business days
- Customs: 3-7 days typical
- Documentation: Commercial invoice required

**Zone 5: Rest of World**
- Transit: 14-21 business days
- Customs: Highly variable
- Tracking: Limited in some countries

## Common Tracking Statuses Explained

### Normal Statuses
- **"Label Created"**: Waiting for carrier pickup (24-48 hrs normal)
- **"In Transit"**: Package moving through network
- **"Out for Delivery"**: Will arrive today
- **"Delivered"**: Check delivery location notes

### Problem Statuses
- **"Exception"**: Delay occurred, check details
- **"Returned to Sender"**: Address issue or refused
- **"Held at Location"**: Customer pickup required
- **"Clearance Delay"**: Customs holding package

## Address Issues & Solutions

### Common Problems
**"Insufficient Address"**
- Missing apartment/suite number
- Fix: Contact customer immediately

**"No Such Number"**
- House number doesn't exist
- Fix: Verify with customer, Google Maps

**"Business Closed"**
- Delivery attempted outside hours
- Fix: Get alternate address or schedule redelivery

**"Access Denied"**
- Gated community/secure building
- Fix: Get access code or alternate delivery

## Shipping Cost Exceptions

### When to Waive Shipping
1. Order error on our part
2. Damaged item replacement
3. Lost package replacement
4. Customer service recovery
5. VIP customer request

### When NOT to Waive
1. Customer changed mind on shipping speed
2. Entered wrong address
3. Wants faster shipping after order placed
4. International duties/taxes
5. Remote area surcharges

## Peak Season Adjustments

### Black Friday - Cyber Monday
- Add 1-2 days to all estimates
- Disable overnight shipping
- Set customer expectations

### December 15-24
- Add 2-3 days to estimates
- Carrier pickups may be delayed
- Tracking updates slower

### January Returns Rush
- Return processing: Add 2 days
- Higher package volume
- Extend return window to 45 days

## Quick Scripts for Common Situations

**Package Shows Delivered But Not Received:**
"I see tracking shows delivery at [time] on [date]. Sometimes packages are placed in unexpected locations. Could you check [common hiding spots]? If you still can't locate it, I'll file a claim immediately and send a replacement."

**Tracking Not Updating:**
"I understand how concerning it is when tracking goes silent. I can see the last scan was [location] on [date]. This usually means [common reason]. If we don't see movement by [date], I'll immediately [solution]."

**Customer Wants Faster Shipping After Ordering:**
"I completely understand wanting your order sooner! Unfortunately, I can't modify shipping speed once an order is in our fulfillment center. However, your order is on track to arrive by [date]. Would you like me to check if I can expedite processing?"

**International Customer Asking About Duties:**
"Import duties and taxes are determined by your country's customs office and are separate from our shipping charges. The amount varies by country and order value. You'll typically pay these to the carrier upon delivery. I recommend checking your country's import regulations for specific rates."
