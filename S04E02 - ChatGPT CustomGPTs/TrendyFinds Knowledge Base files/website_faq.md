# Frequently Asked Questions

## Service & Scheduling

### When do you plough my driveway?
We monitor weather conditions 24/7 and clear driveways when snowfall reaches our trigger depth (5cm for standard seasonal contracts, 3cm for premium packages). Our goal is to have your driveway cleared before 7am on weekdays and 9am on weekends, though we often finish much earlier.

### What if I need my driveway cleared at a specific time?
Premium package customers receive guaranteed clearing by 7am on weekdays. For per-storm customers, we offer early-morning bookings (before 6am same day) based on availability. Commercial clients can arrange custom scheduling.

### Do you work during the night?
Yes! Most of our ploughing happens between 2am-7am so your driveway is clear before you need to leave for work or school. You'll likely sleep through the whole thing.

### What happens if it snows all day?
For heavy, continuous snowfall, we may make multiple passes throughout the day to prevent buildup. This is included in seasonal contracts at no extra charge.

### How do I know when you've been to my property?
We send an email and/or SMS notification after completing service at your property. You can also see the cleared driveway from your window!

## Pricing & Contracts

### How much does seasonal service cost?
Our standard Seasonal Snow Protection Plan is $599 for the season (October 15 - April 15). Premium packages start at $899. Pricing varies based on driveway size and any special requirements.

### Is per-storm service cheaper than a seasonal contract?
It depends on the winter! If we clear your driveway 10+ times (which is typical in most parts of Canada), a seasonal contract saves money. Plus, you'll never worry about booking or availability during storms.

### When do I pay?
Seasonal contracts can be paid in full (with a 5% discount) or split into three monthly payments. Per-storm service is invoiced after each visit with payment due within 7 days.

### What's included in the price?
Standard service includes ploughing your driveway and pushing snow to a designated area on your property. Walkway clearing, ice melt application, and other services are available as add-ons.

### Are there any extra charges?
Extra-large driveways (over 40m length), steep grades (15%+ slope), or emergency same-day bookings may incur additional fees. We'll always inform you of any surcharges before starting service.

## Snow Placement & Property Care

### Where do you put the snow?
We'll discuss snow placement during your initial consultation. Common options include the end of the driveway, side yards, or designated areas away from walkways. We're careful to avoid blocking garage doors, walkways, or drainage areas.

### What if there's nowhere to put the snow?
For properties with limited space, we offer snow hauling and removal services. This is quoted separately based on the amount of snow and disposal requirements.

### Will you damage my driveway or landscaping?
Our experienced operators use professional equipment and techniques to protect your property. We'll note any existing damage during our initial visit. If we do cause damage (which is extremely rare), we'll make it right.

### Do you clear to bare pavement?
We clear to approximately 1-2cm of packed snow, which is standard for residential ploughing and helps protect your driveway surface. For bare pavement (often required for commercial properties), please request this during booking.

## Weather & Storms

### How do you define a "storm" for per-storm billing?
Any snowfall event that requires ploughing counts as one storm/visit. If it snows 5cm overnight, that's one visit. If it continues snowing heavily and requires a second pass later that day, seasonal customers aren't charged extra, but per-storm customers would be billed for the second visit.

### What about freezing rain or ice?
Our service focuses on snow ploughing. For ice control, add our ice melt application service. We'll monitor conditions and recommend service when needed.

### Can you predict how many times you'll need to plough?
Based on historical averages, most areas in our service region require 12-18 ploughing visits per season. Some winters are lighter (8-10 visits) while harsh winters can exceed 20 visits. Seasonal contracts cover unlimited visits.

### What if there's a massive blizzard?
We prioritize seasonal contract customers during major storms, though we work around the clock to serve everyone. Extreme weather may delay our usual timeline, but we'll keep you informed and get to you as soon as safely possible.

Yes, we carry full commercial liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. Documentation is available upon request, especially for commercial and property management clients.

We'll work around vehicles when possible, but for the most thorough clearing, we recommend parking on the street during overnight storms. We'll clear as much as we can access safely.
