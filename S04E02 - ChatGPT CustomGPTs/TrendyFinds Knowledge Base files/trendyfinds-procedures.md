# TrendyFinds Internal Procedures & Policies

## Return Processing Procedures

### Standard Return Process
1. Customer initiates return through website or customer service
2. System generates RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number
3. Prepaid return label sent via email (FedEx or USPS)
4. Customer has 14 days to ship item back
5. Upon receipt, inspect within 24 hours
6. Process refund within 2-3 business days

### Refund Calculation
- Full product price
- Minus return shipping fee ($7.99)
- Minus any promotional discount if returning would break promo threshold
- Original shipping fee is non-refundable

### Exchange Process
1. First exchange: Free shipping both ways
2. Subsequent exchanges: Customer pays shipping
3. Process as priority - ship within 1 business day
4. Include handwritten note apologizing for size issue

## Customer Service Escalation Tiers

### Tier 1: Customer Service Rep Authority
- Refunds up to $50 without manager approval
- Free return labels (waive $7.99 fee) 
- 20% discount codes for service recovery
- Expedite shipping on replacements
- Apply promo codes retroactively (48 hours)

### Tier 2: Supervisor Authority  
- Refunds up to $200
- Accept returns outside 30-day window (up to 60 days)
- Issue store credit for final sale items
- 30% discount codes
- Waive one-time shipping fees

### Tier 3: Manager Authority
- Refunds over $200
- Accept heavily worn/damaged returns
- Issue full refunds without return
- Create custom solutions
- Handle legal threats or BBB complaints

## Special Situations

### Lost/Stolen Packages
**If tracking shows delivered but customer claims not received:**
1. Ask customer to check with neighbors/building
2. Wait 48 hours (packages often appear)
3. If still missing, choose based on customer history:
   - First-time customer: Request filed police report
   - Repeat customer: Send replacement immediately
   - VIP customer (>$1000 annual): No questions asked

### Damaged Items
1. Request photo of damage
2. No need to return item
3. Choose: Full refund or replacement
4. If replacement, upgrade shipping for free
5. Add $10 credit for inconvenience

### Wrong Item Sent
1. Apologize profusely
2. Send correct item via express shipping (free)
3. Include prepaid label for wrong item
4. Add 20% off next order code
5. Follow up within 48 hours

### Price Adjustments
- Within 7 days of purchase: Honor price drop
- 8-14 days: Case by case basis
- After 14 days: Generally decline unless VIP
- During major sales: One adjustment per customer

## Communication Guidelines

### Email Templates

**Delayed Shipping Response:**
"I sincerely apologize for the delay with your order. I can see this should have arrived by [date], and I understand how frustrating this must be. I've immediately [action taken]. You can expect [resolution timeline]. As an apology, I've added [compensation] to your account."

**Return Approved:**
"Your return has been approved! I've emailed your prepaid return label - just print it out and drop your package at any [carrier] location. Once we receive it, your refund of $[amount] will be processed within 2-3 business days. The $7.99 return fee will be deducted from your refund."

**Issue Resolution:**
"Great news! I've resolved this issue for you. Here's what I've done: [specific actions]. You should see [expected result] within [timeframe]. Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

### Tone Guidelines
- Always empathize first
- Use customer's name once per conversation
- Avoid corporate speak
- Be specific with timelines
- Own the problem even if not our fault
