# Snow Management Canada - Service Packages & Pricing

## Residential Services

### Seasonal Snow Protection Plan
**Price: $599/season** (Oct 15 - Apr 15)

**Includes:**
- Unlimited ploughing visits throughout the season
- Clearing when snowfall reaches 5cm or more
- Standard driveway up to 2 car width x 20m length
- Snow pushed to designated area on property
- Email/SMS notification before each visit
- Priority service during major storms

**Best for:** Homeowners who want peace of mind and predictable costs

---

### Per-Storm Service
**Price: $75 per visit**

**Includes:**
- Single driveway clearing
- Same-day service when booked before 6am
- Standard driveway up to 2 car width x 20m length
- 24-hour advance booking available

**Best for:** Homeowners who travel frequently or have flexible schedules

---

### Premium Winter Care Package
**Price: $899/season**

**Includes:**
- Everything in Seasonal Snow Protection Plan
- Walkway and front step clearing
- Ice melt application on walkways
- Clearing when snowfall reaches 3cm or more
- Guaranteed clearing by 7am on weekdays
- Weekly property check during non-snow periods

**Best for:** Elderly residents, busy professionals, or those with mobility concerns
