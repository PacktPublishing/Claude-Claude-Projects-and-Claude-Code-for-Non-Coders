# TrendyFinds Customer FAQ

## Shipping & Delivery

### How long does shipping take?
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days ($5.99, FREE on orders over $75)
- **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days ($14.99)
- **Overnight Shipping**: Next business day if ordered by 2 PM EST ($29.99)
- **International Shipping**: 10-14 business days ($24.99+, varies by location)

### When will I receive my tracking information?
You'll receive tracking via email within 24 hours of shipment. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

### My package shows delivered but I didn't receive it
1. Check with neighbors or building management
2. Look for the package in secure locations
3. Contact us within 48 hours for investigation
4. We'll file a claim and send a replacement

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. International orders may be subject to customs fees.

## Returns & Exchanges

### What is your return policy?
- 30-day return window from delivery date
- Items must be unworn, unwashed, with tags attached
- Original packaging required
- Return shipping fee: $7.99 (deducted from refund)

### Which items are final sale?
- Clearance items (marked "Final Sale")
- Intimates and underwear
- Swimwear
- Earrings and body jewelry
- Items marked down 50% or more

### How do I start a return?
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Click "Return Item" next to the product
4. Print prepaid label
5. Drop off at any shipping location

### How long do refunds take?
- Processing: 2-3 business days after we receive the item
- Bank posting: Additional 5-7 business days
- Total time: 7-10 business days from receipt

### Can I exchange for a different size?
Yes! First exchange is free shipping both ways. Select "Exchange" instead of "Return" when processing.

## Orders & Payment

### Can I modify or cancel my order?
You have 2 hours after placing to modify or cancel. After that, we begin processing and cannot make changes.

### Payment methods accepted
- All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Afterpay (split into 4 payments)
- TrendyFinds Gift Cards

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes! We use SSL encryption and never store your full credit card details.

### Why was my payment declined?
Common reasons:
- Incorrect billing address
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- International transaction blocked by bank

## Products & Sizing

### How do I find my size?
Use our size guide on each product page. Measurements are in inches. If between sizes, we recommend sizing up.

### Size conversions:
- XS = 0-2
- S = 4-6  
- M = 8-10
- L = 12-14
- XL = 16-18
- 2XL = 20-22
- 3XL = 24-26

### Are your products true to size?
Most items run true to size. Check product reviews for specific fit notes from other customers.

### How do I care for my items?
Care instructions are on each product tag. Generally:
- Machine wash cold
- Hang dry recommended
- Iron on low heat
- Dry clean only items marked accordingly

## Account & Rewards

### Do I need an account to order?
No, guest checkout is available. However, accounts allow you to:

- Save addresses

### How does the rewards program work?

- 100 points = $5 reward

### I forgot my password
Click "Forgot Password" at login. You'll receive a reset link within 5 minutes.
